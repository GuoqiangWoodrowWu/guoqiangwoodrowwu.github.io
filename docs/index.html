About me

I am a tenure-track associate professor at School of Software, Shandong University. I received the B.E. degree from Shandong University in 2012. Then, I received my M.S. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences(UCAS) in 2015, supervised by Prof. Baoping Yan. After one year working in the industry, I returned to UCAS and received my Ph.D. degree in 2019, supervised by Prof. Yingjie Tian. From 2019 to 2021, I did my PostDoc research in TSAIL at Tsinghua University, worked with Prof. Jun Zhu.

Research

My research interests include Machine Learning (especially Stastistical Learning Theory), Optimization, and Artificial Intelligence. Recently, I work on learning theory on multi-label learning, hyperparameter optimization and deep learning.
